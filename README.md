# Everything Simply
 
**https://silent-pfil.github.io/everything-simply/**
 
A developmental sandbox series introducing the foundational concepts of the world — one at a time, in the right order, with patience.
 
---

## How It's Built
 
Every sandbox shares the same architecture:
 
- **PulseTock** — a shared heartbeat engine that begins as a pulse and slowly reveals itself as a clock tick across the ten stages
- **Scene** — a persistent night cosmos background that grows incrementally more visible with each stage, fully present at Stage 10
- **Four tiers** — Learn → Explore → Test → Play — scaling from passive observation to active interaction
Phase 1 uses Tiers I, II, and IV. **Tier III activates in Phase 2**, when concepts introduced here are ready to be recalled, not just recognized.
 
There is no right or wrong in Phase 1. Only exposure.

## The Turing Academy
 
These sandboxes were built with a larger question in mind.
 
What if the same patient, ordered, concept-by-concept curriculum a child needs is also exactly what an artificial mind needs — not fine-tuning, not prompt engineering, but a genuine developmental introduction to the world?
 
The **Turing Academy for Man and Machine** is the framework this series sits inside. Phase 1 is the proof of concept. The curriculum, and the question, continue.
